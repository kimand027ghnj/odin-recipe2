Recipe for Sinigang

Description

This sour tamarind-based soup combines beef, vegetables, and a tangy broth for a comforting and flavorful dish. Rooted in pre-colonial times, sinigang reflects the Filipinos' love for hearty and comforting soups. This beef sinigang recipe offers a simple and delicious way to enjoy the flavors of Filipino comfort food at home.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Meat
  • Kangkong
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook meat and other vegetables
  3. Put salt
  4. Cook Rice
